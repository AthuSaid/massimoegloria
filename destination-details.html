Explore Kenya with Us

Massimo e Gloria Safaris Kenya is a Tour Operator Company fully registered and Operating from the Coast of Malindi Town and offers great safaris in Kenya and Tanzania. Our Mission is to Complete our Client’s dream for the African Safari across Kenya and Tanzania, and not to Compete with other Operators. We offer safaris ranging from Budget safari, Mid Range Safari, Lodge Safari, and Luxury Safari. We accept all bookings and customize itineraries to meet all the client’s Requests.

Kenya’s compelling fusion of wildlife safaris, rich tribal cultures, and stunning natural vistas is an irresistible bite for wildlife enthusiasts. From the wildlife-rich Masai Mara to the historical depths of coastal Lamu, Kenya embodies an intriguing blend of natural wonder and cultural depth.
